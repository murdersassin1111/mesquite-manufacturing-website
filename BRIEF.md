# Mesquite Manufacturing Website Brief

## Company Info
- **Name:** Mesquite Manufacturing
- **URL:** mesquitemanufacturing.com
- **Industry:** CNC Manufacturing / Precision Machining
- **Location:** Texas (local, serves broader market)

## Services
1. **Precision CNC Machining** — 4th axis CNC, tolerances ±0.001"–0.005", metals/plastics/composites
2. **Prototype Development** — Rapid prototyping, iterative feedback, cost-effective
3. **Custom Assembly** — Integration, testing, tailored solutions
4. **3D Printing** — Additive manufacturing, fine tolerances, diverse materials

## Key Differentiators
- 4th axis CNC capability (high-volume, lower cost than competitors)
- Precision tolerances as tight as ±0.001"
- Full service: design → prototype → production → assembly
- Customer-centric, tailored approach
- 24/7 operations

## Target Audience
- Engineers and product designers needing precision parts
- Startups needing prototypes
- Companies needing production runs
- Defense/aerospace/automotive/medical industries

## Competitor Reference Sites
- **Xometry** — Platform-style, instant quoting, trust metrics (M+ parts shipped), clean modern design
- **Protolabs** — Rapid prototyping focus, educational content, professional corporate
- **Fictiv** — Modern SaaS-like design, dark themes, strong CTAs
- **Fast Radius** — Clean, data-driven, case studies
- **SendCutSend** — Bold, simple, orange/dark palette

## Design Requirements
- Mobile-responsive, single-page or multi-page
- Strong hero section with CTA
- Services section with clear descriptions
- About section with company story
- Contact form / Request Quote form
- Trust signals (capabilities, certifications, stats)
- Professional photography placeholders (use placeholder services)
- Modern CSS (no frameworks required, but Tailwind OK)
- Must be deployable as static HTML on GitHub Pages
